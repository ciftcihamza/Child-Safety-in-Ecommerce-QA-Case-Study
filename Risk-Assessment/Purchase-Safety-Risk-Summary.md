## Identified Risk Theme
Unauthorized or accidental purchases on shared family devices due to missing purchase protection mechanisms.

## Related Exploratory Findings
- EF-01: One-tap purchase completion with saved user data
- EF-02: No configurable purchase protection settings
- EF-03: Frictionless purchase flow amplifying unauthorized access risk

## Risk Severity
**Critical**

## Justification
- Financial loss risk for users
- High likelihood due to common shared-device usage
- No user-side mitigation available
- Potential customer trust and brand reputation impact

## Affected Scenarios
- Children using shared family devices
- Lost or stolen unlocked phones
- Unintended access by third parties

## High-Level Recommendation
Introduce mandatory purchase confirmation options such as:
- Password or PIN verification
- Biometric confirmation
- Optional child safety or parental control mode
